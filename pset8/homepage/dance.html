Teaching & Choreography

One of my favorite contemporary ballet works, January, 1987, illustrates the Toro Nagashi, a Japanese festival where thousands of candlelit lanterns are floated down river ceremoniously to remember the lives and spirits of lost loved ones.

Over my career, I've taught kids (and adults!) of all ages ranging from elementary school, to pre-professional students. And, they have been all over the place: New England, Texas, Michigan, L.A., Hawai'i... where to next?

My class focuses on preparing the body with a progressive and kinesthetic floor and barre warm-up, culminating with improvisation.
